Bitcoin

Bitcoin is a peer-to-peer electronic cash system that achieves decentralized and transparent transactions through blockchain technology. It was proposed by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2008 and is the first digital currency to successfully apply blockchain technology. Bitcoin adopts the proof-of-work algorithm and uses encryption technology to ensure the security and privacy of transactions.
